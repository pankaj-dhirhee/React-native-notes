LIFE CYCLE PHASES AND METHODS


Life Cycle phase:
* Mounting
* Update
* Unmounting


When Life Cycle will be executed?:

* When Component render first time.
* When Component update with state.
* When Component update with props.
* On Error [Error in Class component].
* When Component unmount.
